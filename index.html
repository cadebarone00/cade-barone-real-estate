THE PROCESS

HOW it WORKS

A step-by-step guide to buying a home.

1

Intro Call

We'll start with a quick 15-minute call to go over your budget, must-haves, preferred areas, and timing — plus make sure we're a great fit to work together.

2

Get Pre-Approved

I'll connect you with a trusted mortgage broker to secure your pre-approval, so you know exactly what you can afford and can act quickly when the right home comes up.

3

Start the Search

This is where the fun begins. We'll tour homes that match your criteria, and I'll share insight on neighbourhoods, pricing, and opportunities you might not have considered.

4

Offer & Keys

When we find the one, I'll negotiate hard on your behalf and guide you through every step of closing — right up to the moment the keys are in your hand.
